The Top 10 Scenic Drives in California

Published on March 15, 2026 by Elena Vance

There is no better way to experience the raw beauty of California than from the driver's seat of a high-performance machine. From the fog-shrouded cliffs of Big Sur to the sun-drenched vineyards of Napa Valley, we've curated the ultimate guide to the state's most iconic roads.

1. Pacific Coast Highway (Hwy 1)

Stretching over 650 miles along the California coastline, the PCH is widely considered one of the most beautiful drives in the world. For the ultimate experience, we recommend renting our **Ferrari 488 Pista Spider**—the open air and the roaring V8 are the perfect companions for the winding turns of Big Sur.

2. Death Valley Scenic Drive

If you prefer a more otherworldly landscape, the drive through Death Valley National Park offers vast salt flats, colorful badlands, and towering sand dunes. The long, straight desert roads are where the **Lamborghini Huracan EVO** truly shines, allowing you to experience its stability and aerospace-inspired technology.

"The road is there to be driven, but the car is there to be felt. Every turn in California tells a different story when you're in the right machine."
